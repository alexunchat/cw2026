1. Development Approach

The Clockwork 2026 roadmap will be executed using a two-pillar approach to deliver measurable business impact while ensuring system stability and scalability.

ü§ñ Strategic Pillar 1: AI-Driven Client Lifecycle

  • Train AI with business rules and custom thresholds
  • Predictive intelligence for at-risk client identification
  • Intelligent practitioner matching and routing
  • Proactive gap detection before issues occur

üöÄ Strategic Pillar 2: Service Line Configuration

Goal: Scale without limits through self-service configuration

  • Self-service configuration of service types
  • Simulation and forecasting before deployment
  • EMR-agnostic architecture (Salesforce optional)
  • Multi-tenant architecture for unlimited scalability
